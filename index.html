Great Software is Discovered.

Your goal is to build a product and charge for it. More customers, more revenue, more growth.

You want impact.

Sustained desirability drives success. Honest, non-bribed word of mouth drives desirability. Making your users honest-to-god better drives word-of-mouth. If you have to copy, it would mean you've already been outdone by what you're copying. The takeaway is simple...

If you want to make an impact, you have to discover what's impactful.
